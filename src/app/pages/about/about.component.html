TRANSFORMING DATA INTO ACTIONABLE INSIGHTS

Our work is inspired by a true understanding of data transformation and a hunger to develop seamless software systems. We are driven by a desire for innovation and a vision to create the perfect combination of analytics and design. We tailor all of our products to suit specific needs and focuses. No two projects are the same, allowing for our entrepreneurial spirit and keen eyes for detail to create the most cutting-edge and solutions.

Over the past few months, we have focused our efforts on the mining industry in Queensland. In collaboration with a major energy corporation, we are currently developing a geological and drilling data solution that will provide engineers with vital insights into their activities. We have identified that there is a vast array of underutilised data in this sector and are looking to expand our solutions into areas such as minerals and infrastructure.

FOUNDED IN 2017 AND BASED IN BRISBANE, AUSTRALIA, WE ARE SUPPORTED BY THE UNIVERSITY OF QUEENSLAND’S ILAB GERMINATE PROGRAM FOR ENTREPRENEURS AND UNEARTHED SOLUTIONS.
